PS5

The PlayStation 5 (PS5 for short) is a stationary game console belonging to the PlayStation brand from the Japanese company Sony, which was launched on the European market on November 19, 2020. It is the successor to the PlayStation 4, with which there is backward compatibility. The PS5 competes with Microsoft's Xbox Series console and Nintendo's Switch. As of December 2023, over 50 million copies have been sold worldwide.
